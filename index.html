About Us

Health begins with knowledge. Our environment has both long and short term affects on our well-being. Here at AirPollution.com, we strive to provide an instant and accurate live air quality report. Our color-coded index is easy to read. Go ahead. Just type a location and check the air quality.

Pollutons

Federal and state funded Air monitoring stations measure particles like; CO: Carbon Monoxide, NO: Nitrc Oxide, NO2: Nitrogen Dioxide, O3: TrioxygenSO2, Sulfur Dioxide, PM2.5: Fine particle matter, PM10: Particle matter, NH3: Ammonium & UV: Ultraviolet radiation. Air samples are taken once every hour, everyday.
